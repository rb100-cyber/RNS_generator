Quotes & Voices

Each voice answers a different investor question. Aim for 2–3 distinct registers. R13 R14 R19

Multi-voice rule: Quote 1 = technical confirmation ("did we hit what we aimed for?"). Quote 2 = commercial momentum ("what does this mean for the thesis?"). Quote 3 (optional) = institutional framing from chair or named external party ("why does this matter?"). Don't stack all answers into a single CEO quote.
